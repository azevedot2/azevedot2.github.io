BEAUTY FOCUS COLLAGEN+

Graphic Design | Social Media | UI | Video

PROJECT DETAILS

Context

Nu Skin is a global beauty and wellness company known for its skincare products, innovative beauty devices, and nutritional supplements—such as Beauty Focus Collagen+, a collagen powder formulated with collagen peptides optimised for beauty.
This project aimed to drive revenue through online advertising of this product.

My role

As part of the Digital Marketing Team at Nu Skin EMEA, I collaborated closely with copywriters, photographers, and content strategists to develop the visual concept and produce all static and motion assets for Google Ads and social media campaigns.
