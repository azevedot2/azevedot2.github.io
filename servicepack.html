SERVICEPACK

UI Design | Front-End Development | Graphic Design | 3D

Project Details

Context

SERVICEPACK is a trusted provider of A/V equipment rentals and digital solutions for events, with over 20 years of industry experience.
Despite a recent brand refresh, the company struggled with market awareness and distinction. As social media and digital marketing have become increasingly essential over the past decade, strengthening the brand’s online presence emerged as a top priority. To remain competitive and enhance visibility, SERVICEPACK set out to develop a comprehensive suite of digital assets, launch a modern website, and boost its social media channels.

My role

Graphic Design (digital and printed), Web Design & Development, Content Creation for Social Media and 3D event visualization.

Key tools

Adobe Creative Suite / HTML / CSS / Javascript / Blender

Website development process:

1. Understand business and client needs:
Meet with stakeholders to understand their vision, expectations and goals.

2. Visual direction:
Become familiar with the corporate brand and design guidelines / Analyse competitor websites and similar business for inspiration and insights. Define the tone and style.

3. Information Architecture & Design:
Define maps, pages, create user flow charts, design page mockups & validate with stakeholders.

4. Development & maintenance:
Using open-source front-end framework Bootstrap to accelerate development by taking advantage of its pre-built responsive grid system, ready-to-use UI components, and built-in JavaScript plugins.
