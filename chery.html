PROJECT DETAILS

Context

Salvador Caetano Auto Africa is the African subsidiary of the Salvador Caetano Group focused on auto distribution and retail in markets with large growth potential. The Company is the exclusive auto distributor of Chery.

My role

As part of the Digital Transformation Office at Salvador Caetano, my role as a UI/UX Specialist was to design the official Chery website for the African markets. This involved applying the global design system and guidelines, suggesting improvements, working closely with the web development team, and validating all stages with stakeholders.
