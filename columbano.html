PROJECT DETAILS

Context

“Columbano” was a communication design project for a temporary art exhibition in 2012 at Museu de Grão Vasco, Portugal, aimed to present 55 pieces of Columbano Bordalo Pinheiro’s painting collection.

My role

Design of all communication materials.

Key tools

Adobe Illustrator | Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Flash | Adobe Premiere Pro

Main Cover Painting

“Chaleira de Cobre”, 1920 Oil on Wood Painting H.22 cm x W.27 cm Columbano Bordalo Pinheiro (1857-1929)
