INSONIA

Front-end development

PROJECT DETAILS

Context

Insonia captures the world through a creative lens, specializing in photography and video for the corporate, advertising, and e-commerce worlds.

In 2016, the company embarked on a bold reimagining of its brand. With a fresh new logo and a revitalized identity, a sleek, modern website was the next natural step - a digital space as sharp and dynamic as the stories Insonia tells.

My role

Website development using the open-source front-end framework Bootstrap to accelerate development by taking advantage of its pre-built responsive grid system, ready-to-use UI components, and built-in JavaScript plugins.
